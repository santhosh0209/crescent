Crescent University

B.S. Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and technology is a renowned Quality Leadership Institution located at the greenest spot of Chennai near Tambaram. Through our long history of 36 years of excellence, the Institution has offered access to a wide range of academic opportunities.

With 46 programmes, grouped under 12 different Schools, 27 Undergraduate programmes, 19 Postgraduate programmes, and Ph.D. (in all the departments), this institution is a rising stalwart in higher education with promising Quality, Security and Placement.
